The question of how much these two other-worldly players inspire each other is an interesting one, and it's tempting to imagine Messi sitting at home on Tuesday night, watching Ronaldo destroying Atletico, angrily glaring at the TV screen and growling: "Right, I'll show him!" Rather than being a better player than Ronaldo, Messi's main motivations - according to the people who are close to him - are being the best possible version of Lionel Messi, and winning as many trophies as possible.
